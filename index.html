Our Mission

All Nation is not a pilot. We are a proven institution. For nearly three decades, we have been the backbone of Mayfield — delivering municipal projects, creating jobs, and uplifting our community.


We are Elsie Mamabolo and Msawenkosi Xulu — neighbours, partners, and community anchors. Together, we have self-funded initiatives, built trust, and earned the respect of the local municipality and the people of Mayfield.


Now, we seek to expand our impact. We call on government, municipalities, and corporate South Africa to partner with us. We have the track record. We have the trust. We have the capacity to deliver.


"We are building a future that the next generation will be proud to inherit."

What We Deliver

Municipal Infrastructure

Roads, drainage, public space improvements, and community facilities in Mayfield Ext. 1 and surrounding areas.

Youth Empowerment

Skills development, mentorship, and job placement for young people in Mayfield — funded by directors personally.

Community Welfare

Food security programs, elderly support, and social welfare initiatives that have supported 100+ families annually.

Job Creation

Over 500 local jobs created through municipal projects and community initiatives over the past 25+ years.

Social Development

Programs that promote social wellbeing, community cohesion, and dignity for all residents of Mayfield.

Project Management

End-to-end project delivery from planning to execution, with a proven track record of excellence and accountability.
